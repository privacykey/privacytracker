# Severity scale

Apple's privacy labels group every data category into one of three severities.
The visual treatment is driven by `SEVERITY_CONFIG` in `lib/privacy-meta.ts`
and the matching `.severity-*` classes in `app/globals.css`.

## Why each severity has an icon AND a label

Per the project's WCAG 1.4.1 contract (no semantic by colour alone), each
severity carries a glyph (👁 / 🔗 / 🔓) next to its label and tint. Toggle the
toolbar to **High contrast** to confirm the glyphs and outlines stay legible
without the tint doing the work.
